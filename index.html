Our Story

Since the beginning, the family-owned La Pizza de Paeso in La Casa has kept it simple. Using the best (mostly organic) pizza ingredients, it offers only one flavor of pizza per day—always a vegetarian selection.
The secret to the La Pizza de Paeso's success and customers lining up outside before the doors even open starts with quality. Incredible dough is made with organic flour, while ingredients are chosen at the very peak of production.

About La Pizza del paeso

La Pizza Del Paeso might be one of the most recognisable icons of La Casa. Whichever way you slice it, La Pizza Del Paeso is an industry leader in the quick service pizza game. The outlet opened its doors in 1994 in La Casa, De Papel, and rose quickly in popularity. Serving hot oven-fresh pizza with its signature all-natural tomato sauce and using only the freshest ingredients, La Pizza Del Paeso has established itself at the top of the industry and is easily one of the first choices in quick service pizza in La Casa.
